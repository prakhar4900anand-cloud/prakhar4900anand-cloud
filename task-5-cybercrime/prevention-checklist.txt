PART B: PREVENTION CHECKLIST


Online Safety - Check List To Avoid Danger

1. Never Give Out Any OTP's Or UPI PIN #’s To Anyone
2. Always Confirm The UPI ID Prior To Making A Payment
3. Don’t Click On Unknown Or Suspicious Links
4. Don’t Accept Unknown Payment Requests
5. Always Use Trusted and Legitimate Applications For Transactions
6. Enable SMS & Application Alerts For All Transactions
7. Use Strong, Unique Passwords
8. Immediately Report Fraud

Report Cyber Crime At: https://www.cybercrime.gov.in  
Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930
